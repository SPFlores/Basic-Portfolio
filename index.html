About Me

Samantha Flores, up and coming hopeful keyboard maestro, graduated from LMU with a BS in Biology in May of 2014. Since then, she has been a lab quality tester for things as diverse as flavorings for your favorite foods, nutritional supplements to help you live a healthier life, and medical devices that are used daily to save lives. She's coming back for another round of schooling because of her lifelong desire to learn and her interest in the future. While she briefly considered minoring in Computer Science in college, most of this information is new to her.
